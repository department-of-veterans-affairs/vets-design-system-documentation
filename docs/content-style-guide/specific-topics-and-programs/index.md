# Specific topics and programs

This section provides guidance for creating consistent, plain-language content about particular VA topics and programs. Using consistent terminology helps establish trust with Veterans, service members, and family members across multiple communication channels and languages.

## How we develop this guidance

The guidance in this section draws from:

- VA content and plain language standards
- Search and analytics research findings
- Input from VA subject matter experts
- Feedback from Veterans and service members through usability research and surveys

## What each entry includes

Each topic entry provides:

- **Considerations** for writing about the topic
- **Related terminology** and proper usage
- **Obsolete terms** and recommended alternatives
- **Resources** including published examples and research data
