# For content writers

The VA.gov content style guide helps writers create better content, focused on usage rather than bureaucracy.

## Getting started resources

### Content principles

Our [content principles](/docs/content-style-guide/content-principles) guide the voice and tone of everything we write.

### Word list

The [word list](/docs/content-style-guide/word-list) provides VA.gov's in-house style for consistent word usage and labeling across the platform.

### SEO writing

[Writing for SEO](/docs/content-style-guide/writing-for-seo) offers basic tips and best practices for making content discoverable through search.

### Design system documentation

The [component documentation guide](./component-documentation-guide) provides guidance for writing content that appears on the VA Design System website itself.

## Key style guide sections

The style guide covers:

- [Abbreviations and acronyms](/docs/content-style-guide/abbreviations-and-acronyms)
- [Alternative text for images](/docs/content-style-guide/alternative-text-for-images)
- [Plain language](/docs/content-style-guide/plain-language) (active voice, contractions, simple words, short sentences)
- [Button labels](/docs/content-style-guide/button-labels)
- [Error and alert messaging](/docs/content-style-guide/error-messages)
- [Health content](/docs/content-style-guide/health-content) and destigmatizing language
- [Links](/docs/content-style-guide/links), [punctuation](/docs/content-style-guide/punctuation-and-formatting), and formatting
- [Page titles and section titles](/docs/content-style-guide/page-titles-and-section-titles)
- [Government forms](/docs/content-style-guide/government-forms)

## Getting content guidance early

The Sitewide Content and Information Architecture team offers support for new products and existing refinements. Teams can submit an intake form to ensure content meets VA.gov standards for accuracy, consistency, accessibility, and equity.

## Governance vs. style guide

For VA web policies and governance (VA Handbook 6102), visit the VA Web Governance site separately. This style guide focuses on writing standards only.

## Feedback and updates

Writers can sign up for email updates and submit feedback to vawebdesign@va.gov.
