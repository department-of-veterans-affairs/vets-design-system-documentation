# Help users to...

Patterns for guiding users through complex tasks and processes. These patterns help users:

- Check eligibility for benefits
- Navigate multi-step applications
- Review and verify their information
- Recover from errors
- Manage their benefits and tools

Browse the patterns in this section to find guidance for supporting your users through their journey.
